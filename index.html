About music

« Ardour » Teebs

As music from the Los Angeles area thrives and swells across the globe, one of it's most beloved and deeply rooted members steps out with an LP full of infectious melodies, subtle hip-hop and a kaleidoscope of sound. Music for the imagination.

« Sad clown bad summer » Atmosphere

Just in time for some Summer fun Slug and Ant drop an Atmosphere appetizer while they put the finishing touches on their 6th official studio album, When Life Gives You Lemons, due early next year. Widely known for their countless rare and limited tour only recordings the Sad Clown Bad Dub series has become a staple in Atmosphere's 10 year recording career.

« Third eye vision » Hieroglyphics

A hip-hop supergroup doesn't just form overnight; rather, the artists have to prove over time that they can vibe together, both in the booth and on the mic. Take a look at early Del and Souls of Mischief albums, and you'll find members of Hieroglyphics all over the credits
